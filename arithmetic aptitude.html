Question:

1. A train running at the speed of 60 km/hr crosses a pole in 9 seconds. What is the length of the train?

A. 120 metres
B. 180 metres
C. 324 metres
D. 150 metres
Explanation:
Speed = 60 x (5/18) m/sec = 50 m/sec.
Length of the train = (Speed x Time) = 50 x 9 m = 150 m.
